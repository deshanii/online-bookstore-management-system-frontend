Frequently Asked Questions

What type of books do you sell?

We only sell brand new English non-fiction and fiction books. We do not sell educational books or Sinhala/Tamil books.

What are your delivery charges?

We charge a flat rate of Rs.350 for delivery. If your order value is above Rs. 5,000 you get free delivery island wide!

Do you accept COD (Cash On Delivery)?

Yes. We accept CoD. You can select CoD at the checkout page.

Which payment methods are accepted?

We accept VISA, Mastercard, AMEX and all the popular online payment methods. We accept Cash-On-Delivery for any order amount. You can also choose to pay in instalments via MintPay. We do not accept Direct Bank Transfers.

Do you maintain a stock of books in Sri Lanka?

Yes we do. We have our own inventory of books in Sri Lanka. That’s why our customers love purchasing from us. We deliver books as soon as you order from us.

How long will delivery take?

We deliver within 2-5 working days throughout Sri Lanka.

Where is your bookstore?

We do not have a physical bookstore. We are purely online.

Are these books brand new or used?

All the books are BRAND NEW bestsellers. We do not sell used books.

Do you sell reprints or original books?

We don’t sell reprints to avoid copyright infringement and to uphold the quality of our products. Instead, we directly source books from authorized publishers, ensuring each purchase comes with that assurance.

Can I come and pickup a book from your warehouse?

We only do deliveries at the moment so you cannot pick up orders from our warehouse. But to make it up to you, we deliver island wide.

Can you get me a book that is not listed on your website?

Sorry. We can’t do customized orders because it’s super costly for us. But you can request a book here. 

Do I have to create an account before ordering?

Yes. You have to create an account. You can simply add a username and a password during checkout. Or else, click on Login/Register button on the top right corner. 

Once you have an account with us, you can easily purchase products in future. 

Not only that. You can manage your orders, add products to a wishlist and do so much more!

Do you have a loyalty program?

Yes we do. For every Rs.100 you spend, you will earn Rs.1. You can learn more about it here.

What are your business hours?

We are open Monday-Friday from 9am to 5pm. We are closed on Mercantile and Poya holidays.

Can we place an order through DMs/WhatsApp/Messenger/phone call?

We don’t take any orders through messaging services or over the phone. All orders must be placed through our website www.jumpbooks.lk

Can you tell me when a particular book will be back in stock?

We can’t always give you exact restock dates for books. But you can sign up for back-in-stock notifications by searching for a book on our website and entering your name + email address. Then you’ll be automatically notified when the book is back!

Can you tell me if a particular book is available?

Our website is updated in real-time. If a book is available, you can buy it immediately! If it is not available, it will be labelled as “out of stock” on the website.

Can I reserve a book?

We don’t take reservations as we only get a limited number of copies. Sorry.

Can I sell my book at your store?

Yes, we love to support local authors/publishers! If you would like us to sell your book, contact us on +94767271345 to find out our terms of sale and further details.

Can we return/exchange a book if we don’t like it?

We don’t accept returns/exchanges if you change your mind. We only accept returns/exchanges if a book was damaged during delivery.

Can I cancel an order that I placed?

You may cancel an order if it hasn’t been shipped yet. Once shipped, usually on the next working day, it is too late to cancel.
